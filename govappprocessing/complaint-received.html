Handover to Live Agent

A member of the public decides to complain via live chat about the loud noise coming from the KTV Hotpot premise.

The live chat, powered by AI, suggests to chat to a live agent and seamlessly hands over the conversation for a live agent to engage.

Conversation Transcript & Context

Carlos the live agent is able to get context via the conversation transcript.

Live Sentiment Analysis

Carlos has a useful "sentiment" indicator that can quickly update him on the possible sentiment of the person engaging on live chat.

This sentiment updates in real-time in response to the tone of the active conversation.

Real-Time Langugage Translation

Carlos can turn on real-time language translation in Dynamics 365 to communicate with customers in different languages seamlessly.

By enabling real-time translation, agents like Carlos can view and respond to messages in their preferred language, while customers receive the messages in their chosen language. This removes language barriers and improves customer satisfaction.

Escalate to Video & Voice

Carlos can at a click of a button esclate to voice or video call from a live chat session.

This helps resolve enquiries faster and in a more personal manner.

Share an Instant Teams Meeting Link

Having a longer form meeting over Teams is also possible thanks to the native integration with Microsoft Teams.

Carlos can generate an instant Teams meeting link without switching away from the conversation, and provide the link to the customer, all within the same unified interface.

AI Generated Conversation Summary

Agents like Carlos can quickly recap and understand the context of an ongoing chat or a transcribed voice conversation.

Link Convo to Contact & Customer 360 View

Carlos can easily associate or "link" a conversation to a new or existing contact within the unified interface.

By linking a conversation to a contact record, agents can gain additional context by viewing more contact information and related records such as cases etc.

Copilot AI Assistant

An AI assistant within the unified interface helps users like Carlos to get answers quickly, as well as draft and polish content faster.

Guided by Agent Scripts

Agent scripts provide step-by-step guidance to Carlos on what to do and what to say during the conversation, ensuring that he shares accurate and consistent information.

Create Case from Convo & Wrap Up

As the conversation is about to wrap up, Carlos can easily Create a Case for the purpose of investigating the complaint, as well as capture any relevant notes.

Seamless Outlook Integration

The complaint is emailed to Carlos personal email account. But not a problem!

Carlos can easily sync that email to Dynamics 365 and link it to the related case, thanks to the seamless integration with Microsoft Outlook.

Schedule a Work Order for Amy

Carlos is now ready to schedule a work order to investigate the complaint.

He's able to do so in the same interface, creating a work order from the case and using the schedule board to book available "field officers" who can perform inspections.
