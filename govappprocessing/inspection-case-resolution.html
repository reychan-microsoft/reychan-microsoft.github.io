Mobile Inspection

Amy checks her mobile app and sees that she has a scheduled visit to a premise.

The app enables her to change her status, navigate to the destination, conduct an inspection based on a checklist, and record notes and evidence.

She also has an AI assistant that can give her a summary of the work order and provides suggested actions.

Prohibition Order

After investigating the matter, KTV Hotpot is cleared of any wrongdoing and it's attributed to a rowdy and wildly disruptive patron.

A prohibition order is issued and this order can be accessed by Amy in the mobile app.

The public can also view prohibition orders on the government online portal.

Email with AI & Case Resolution

Carlos is able to monitor the progress of the Case and related Work Order within the same interface and get Case and Work Order summaries generated by AI.

Once it's ready to be resolved, Carlos is able to draft an email to the person who lodged the complaint using Copilot in Dynamics 365 to improve the tone, clarity, and grammar of the email.

The case is now resolved!
