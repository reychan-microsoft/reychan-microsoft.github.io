The Unified Interface

Licensing Officer Carlos uses a clean and simple application interface that has been tailored for his role and configured to optimise staff productivity and efficiency.

The navigation menu allows him to pin and access recent records, with the relevant records all within easy reach.

Global Search & Quick Create

Carlos can easily search for records within the system using the global search bar, no matter where he is in the app.

As he handles walk-in enquiries and applications, he can quickly initiate a new record using the Quick Create button in the global header bar while the application is open in front of him.

Teams Collaboration & Copilot

Thanks to the native integration of Microsoft Teams within Dynamics 365, Carlos and his team can now enjoy a seamless collaboration experience over data and records.

Additionally, the Copilot experience in Dynamics 365 harnesses the power of AI to enable Carlos to ask questions and receive answers based on system data and knowledge.

Dashboards & Reporting

Carlos can view a range of system dashboard and reports providing insight and visibility to day to day operations.

Dynamics 365 also allows you to embed your existing Power BI reports, bringing to life powerful custom visualizations and interactive dashboards featuring data from various data sources.

Visualise Records on a Map

Carlos can benefit from a visual representation of key records, such as applications and contacts, which are searchable on a map.

This provides him with valuable insights into the local area surrounding an application and the potential impact of a liquor license.

Views & Filters

Carlos has the ability to view records through tailored "Views" which display lists of records and provide filtering options by columns and visual charts.

Additionally, thanks to the seamless integration with Microsoft Excel, it is possible to export these views for further analysis.

Processing the Application with Guided Workflows

Carlos is guided by a business process flow which guides him through the steps of processing an application, from data entry to approval.

He can also track payment receipts and assessment criteria. Now processing an application can be done consistently with accuracy and efficiency while providing a streamlined and user-friendly experience.

Track Application Documents

With Dynamics 365, you can easily track and manage your documents or files within an application record.

You can upload documents or capture documents from the online self-service portal from the Dynamics 365 interface, while taking advantage of native SharePoint integration.

Enforce Conditions with Conditions Library

Carlos is able to add conditions to be enforced on the application from a searchable Conditions Library.

This Conditions Library is a "canvas" Power App that's able to easily be embedded within the Dynamics 365 interface.

Referrals to External Agencies

Carlos can create referrals effortlessly from within the application and collaborate with external agencies for additional input.

Automated workflows can draft emails and generate documents to be sent to external agencies who can add their own conditions through an online portal.

Public Advertisements

Carlos can easily switch on a button to invite members of the public to provide feedback in what is commonly referred to as "public advertisments".

The public can voice their concerns and objections about an application, such as a liquor license, through the online portal.

License Application Approved

Carlos is guided by the business process flow to complete the application process.

The application is determined to be approved, and an automated workflow drafts and sends an email with the approval letter to the successful applicant.
